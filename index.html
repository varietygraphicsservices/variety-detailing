Common Questions

Questions, answered.

How much does mobile auto detailing cost in San Diego?
Full detail packages start at $249, paint correction at $349, and professional ceramic coating at $599. Exact pricing depends on vehicle size and condition — our virtual concierge gives an instant, accurate quote in under 2 minutes, 24/7.
Do you come to my location?
Yes — we're fully mobile. We bring water, power, and professional equipment to your home or office anywhere in San Diego County, including La Jolla, Del Mar, Carlsbad, Encinitas, Coronado, and Point Loma.
How long does a ceramic coating last?
Our professional-grade coatings last 3–5+ years with proper maintenance. Every coating includes full paint decontamination and a single-stage polish before application.
What happens if I call while you're working on a car?
You'll receive a text back within seconds — automatically. Our virtual concierge continues the conversation, quotes your vehicle, and books your slot. You never wait.
What's the difference between a car wash and a professional detail?
A car wash removes surface dirt. A professional detail decontaminates the paint with clay bar treatment, machine-polishes imperfections, deep cleans and sanitizes the interior, and protects every surface — restoring the vehicle as close to showroom condition as possible.
